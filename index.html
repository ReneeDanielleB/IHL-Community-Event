World Suicide Prevention Day

An event hosted by IHL in collaboration with Hanwell Community

About

Ihl & the Hanwell Community members have come together in order to raise awareness about World Suicide Prevention Day.

As mental health is a very important topic in every community & has had great impact on many lives, we realise that many have lost loved ones or even battle with this struggle themselves.
We hope to use this opportunity to encourage those who have been affected by this and raise awareness in the hopes that we can prevent this.
